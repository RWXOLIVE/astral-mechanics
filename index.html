Pokemon Astral Emerald - Mechanic Changes

General Changes Status Effects Ability Changes New Moves & Abilities Item Reworks Tera Type Scientists Level Cap

General Changes

  • Battle style is set mode by default.
  • EVs removed.
  • Wild double battles 12%.
  • Safari Balls = 100% catch rate.
  • Bag is inaccessible in trainer battles.
  • Gym battles auto-heal you before battle.
  • Most TMs are single use.
  • Tutors replaced by TMs/TRs.
  • HMs don't need to be taught — badge + HM is enough.

Status Effects

  • Frostbite: 1/16 HP damage + special move damage reduction. Ice types immune.
  • Critical hit chance: 1/24 → 1/16. Damage unchanged.

Ability Changes/Uncommon info

  • Disguise: No damage when broken.
  • Magma Armor: Prevents critical hits.
  • Moody: May raise accuracy and evasion.
  • Synchronize: 50% chance to match nature.
  • Protean/Libero: No longer changes once per battle.
  • Super Luck: 60%/20% held item rate increase.
  • Harvest: 50% chance to encounter grass types.
  • Reckless: Normal/Fighting moves now hit ghost types with this ability.
  • Weather abilities: Now permanent.

New Moves & Abilities

  • Floral Payload (Ability): Boosts Grass moves by 1.5x.
  • Blazing Soul (Ability): Give Fire moves +1 priority at full HP.
  • Lunar Veil (Ability): Boosts Dark & Ghost moves by 1.2x.
  • Solar Veil (Ability): Boosts Fire & Ground moves by 1.2x.
  • Aqua Fang: Like Psychic Fangs + 10% flinch/defense drop.
  • Venom Drain: Giga Drain clone, 75 BP, 10% poison chance.
  • Tectonic Rage: Flare Blitz clone.
  • Frost Blast: Ice-type Blast Burn clone.

Item Reworks

  • Consumed/removed battle items are not restored after battle.
  • Utility Umbrella: Ignores rain/sun effects.
  • Fishing: Always guaranteed without timing.

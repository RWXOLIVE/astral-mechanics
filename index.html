Pokemon Astral Emerald Changes

Last update 13/03/26

General Changes

  • Battle style is set mode by default.
  • EVs removed.
  • Wild double battles 5%.
  • Safari Balls = 100% catch rate.
  • Bag is inaccessible in trainer battles.
  • Gym battles auto-heal you before battle.
  • HMs do not need to be taught; badge + HM is enough.
  • Consumed/removed battle items are not restored after battle.

Status Effects

  • Frostbite: 1/16 HP damage + special move damage reduction. Ice types immune.
  • Critical hit chance: 1/24 -> 1/16. Damage unchanged.
  • Paralysis: 75% speed reduction instead of 50%.

Ability Changes

  • Disguise: No damage when broken.
  • Magma Armor: Prevents critical hits.
  • Moody: May raise accuracy and evasion.
  • Synchronize: 50% chance to match nature.
  • Protean/Libero: Type changes once per switch (Player) Type changes freely (AI)
  • Unseen Fist/Piercing Drill: 1/4 Damage through protect (Player) Functions before the nerf. No damage reduction(AI)
  • Super Luck: 60%/20% held item rate increase.
  • Gale Wings: Reverted back to how it was in Gen 6.
  • Harvest: 50% chance to encounter grass types.
  • Reckless: Normal/Fighting moves now hit ghost types with this ability.
  • Illuminse: Lowers Special Attack on switch in.
  • Water Compaction: Water Immunity.
  • Weather abilities: Now permanent.

New Moves and Abilities

  • Floral Payload (Ability): Boosts Grass moves by 1.5x.
  • Blazing Soul (Ability): Give Fire moves +1 priority.
  • Lunar Veil (Ability): Boosts Dark and Ghost moves by 1.2x.
  • Solar Veil (Ability): Boosts Fire and Ground moves by 1.2x.
  • Striker (Ability): Boosts kicking moves by 1.2x.
  • Luchador's Pride (Ability): Boosts speed after a KO.
  • Echoing Alloy (Ability): Turns sound-based moves into Steel type moves.
  • Surge Cutter (Ability): Swift Swim + Sharpness.
  • Aqua Fangs(Move): Like Psychic Fangs + 10% flinch/defense drop.
  • Scale Chomp(Move): 20% speed boost by 1 stage and 10% chance to lower Defense.
  • Venom Drain(Move): Giga Drain clone, 75 BP, 10% poison chance.
  • Tectonic Rage(Move): Flare Blitz clone.
  • Frost Blast(Move): Ice-type Blast Burn clone.
  • Volt Volley(Move): 10% chance to paralyze and flinch. Boosted by Striker.

Item Rework

  • Utility Umbrella: Ignores rain/sun effects: for both offense and defense, i.e Rain is up, Arcanine is holding the Utility Umbrella, it would act as if Rain is not up.

Quality Of Life

  • Fishing: Always guaranteed without timing.
  • Built in calc: Built in calc that keeps track of trick room, screens, tailwind, stat buffs/debuffs. Also keeps track of the 9th damage roll.
  • Quick Menu: Press L and it brings up a menu that lets you access the PC, Heal, Tutor moves, Delibird Deliveries, Poke Rider, Hatch Eggs and more.
  • Pokemon Following: Pokemon will follow you in the overworld, isnt that cool.

New Starting Statuses.

  • Aurora Veil: Perma & Removeable.
  • Magma Storm: Perma & 5 turns.
  • Leech Seed: Reapplies after every swap.
  • Arena Trap + Shadow Tag: Prevents Swapping.
  • Gravity: Perma & 5 turns.
  • Fog: Weather Effect from Gen 4 :).
